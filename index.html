Comparing school absencies between NYC boroughs

By Leonardo Lopez Carreno

April 25, 2022

Recently, The New York Post released a report that showed that chronic absenteeism in NYC has risen up to 40% during the last academic year. Chronic absenteeism, meaning when a student misses 15 days or more during an academic year, has been a problem in New York for years, but one that has been severely impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. A closer look at the attendance data from the 2020-2021 academic year can show us how this issue has impacted NYC boroughs at different levels.
